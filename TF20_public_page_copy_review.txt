SECTION 2
Mission Statement

Disciplined operations without losing the reason people show up.

Task Force 20 is built for players who want structured missions, clear expectations, and a command environment that respects preparation. The unit blends special operations, Ranger assault capability, aviation support, and staff sections into one coordinated force.

Members are expected to communicate clearly, follow the chain of command, train for assigned roles, and contribute to a community where standards and teamwork matter.

SECTION 4
Requirements

Minimum expectations before you apply.

Game and Mods
Legal copy of Arma 3, ability to install required Steam Workshop mods, and a stable setup for modded operations.

Voice and Discord
Working microphone, Discord access, and willingness to use structured voice communication during events.

Availability
Reasonable attendance for operations, training, interviews, and required onboarding events.

Conduct
Maturity, teamwork, respect for chain of command, and the ability to follow instructions in structured missions.

Training
Completion of orientation, modpack setup, basic infantry familiarization, radio procedures, and role-specific courses.

Honesty
Accurate application answers, clear scheduling disclosures, and no hidden banned or do-not-rehire history.

SECTION 10
FAQ

Questions before you apply.

Do I need MILSIM experience?
No. New players can join if they are willing to learn and complete the training pipeline.

Can I be a pilot immediately?
Aviation roles depend on unit need, training completion, qualification status, and leadership approval.

How often do you operate?
The unit is structured around planned operations, training blocks, and recruit onboarding events.

Do I have to attend every event?
No, but consistent communication and attendance accountability are expected.

What mods are required?
The current modpack is provided during onboarding and updated through official unit channels.

Do you use Discord?
Yes. Discord is the primary identity and communication entry point for applicants.
