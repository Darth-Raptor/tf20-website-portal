Billet Model

Separate from rank

Members have one current rank, one primary unit assignment, one primary billet, optional secondary billets, optional staff-section assignments, and multiple qualifications.

Training and Qualifications

Courses are not qualifications

Recruit Training Pipeline

Orientation, modpack/server setup, conduct expectations, infantry familiarization, radio procedures, ACE medical familiarization, and final recruit evaluation.

Qualification Approval

Instructors record results and recommend qualifications. Training authority approves routine qualifications and manages expirations.

Expiration Rules

Combat and aviation qualifications: 180 days. Leadership and instructor qualifications: 365 days. Basic onboarding: no expiration.

Emergency only
