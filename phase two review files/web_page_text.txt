## **Task Force 20** is an Arma 3 real-sim unit that focuses on realism, tactics, and immersion. With detailed and immersive missions built in Eden and utilizing mods that take gameplay deeper than the simple "ace interact", our goal is to provide the most accurate recreation of special operations possible in Arma 3. ##

---


### __**WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT**__ ###
**1. Our focus on realism**


We use a mod built specifically for Task Force 20 that enables us to run single missions over multiple play sessions by saving player and vehicle locations, inventories, health/damage, and more. We also prioritize real-world tactics and don't sacrifice realism for easier game play.


**2. Our flexible "op" times**


The Task Force operates around the central time zone, and on average, our operations are on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays around 1900 CST. What makes us different is that our units can schedule missions at times that work best for them, rather than being locked into a set schedule.


**3. Deployment Rotations**


We don't do "one-off" missions, and things will never get repetitive. Campaigns will be continually persistent, meaning logistics such as ammo count or medical supplies matter from mission to mission just as much as completing the objective. Then, once a campaign is finished, the Task Force will rotate "home". The teams will have time to train, learn new skills, test out new gear and mods, and most of all take a break, all while the next campaign is being put together in the background.
